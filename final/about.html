Kaitlyn Ishida

Kaitlyn Ishida is currently a Junior studying at the Leeds School of Business at the University of Colorado Boulder

Hello! My name is Kaitlyn Ishida and I am currently a junior at the Leeds School of Business at the University of Colorado Boulder. I am currently pursuing my bachelor's degree in Business Administration with an emphasis in Marketing. Additionally, I am in the process of completing a minor in Creative Technology & Design, and a certificate in Global Business. I will be studying abroad in Barcelona, Spain in the Spring of 2022, and I plan on graduating in the Fall of 2022.

At CU Boulder, I am currently involved in a variety of clubs and organizations. I am a member of the Diverse Scholars Program and Multicultural Business Students Association at Leeds, and I also participate in an all-female club called Women In Business.

I was born and raised in Aurora, Colorado, and I knew that since visiting CU (my father's alma matter) & the city of Boulder from a young age that I would eventually call Boulder home! Before coming to CU I was a student at Cherokee Trail High School where I was involved in many different clubs and activities including:

  • National Honor Society participant (2017-2019)
  • DECA Competitor (2016-2019)
  • DECA Officer Team — Community Service Coordinator (2018-2019)
  • Principal's Honor Roll (2015-2019)
  • National Hispanic Scholar (2019)

  • Service: Annual volunteer for Backpacks for the Homeless Denver, Organized multiple clothing drives for the Salvation Army
